About Us

Learn More About Our Mission and Vision

Our Mission

At Avian Diversity, our mission is to raise awareness about the rich birdlife in the Central Province of Sri Lanka. We are dedicated to educating the public about the importance of conservation and the role each species plays in our ecosystem.

Our Vision

We envision a world where the beauty and importance of bird species are recognized and preserved. Through our website, we aim to provide valuable information and resources that contribute to the protection and appreciation of avian biodiversity.

Who We Are

Our team is made up of passionate bird enthusiasts, conservationists, and web developers. We combine our expertise to create an engaging and educational platform for anyone interested in learning more about the unique bird species of Sri Lanka.
